# Hello, World — A New Beginning

This is the first post on this little corner of the internet.

I've wanted to start a personal blog for a long time. Not because the world needs another blog — but because I've come to believe that writing is a form of thinking, and thinking deserves a place to settle.

## Why a Blog?

In an age of fleeting tweets and endless feeds, a blog feels almost like an act of resistance. It's slow. It's deliberate. There's no algorithm trying to optimize your attention, no notification badge demanding a response.

Just words on a page.

## What to Expect

This space will hold whatever I find worth writing about:

- **Medicine** — clinical reflections, interesting cases, things I'm learning
- **Technology** — tools, workflows, software that shapes how we work
- **Life** — the ordinary, the surprising, the things worth remembering

## A Simple Commitment

I have only one rule for this blog: write honestly. No SEO optimization, no trying to sound smarter than I am, no performance.

If something here resonates with you — or if you disagree strongly — I'd love to hear from you.

Until next time.

*— GyhMed*
